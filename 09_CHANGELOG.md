# CHANGELOG

Append-only upstream update ledger for the Operative Kernel. `curate-updates` uses this file as the authoritative review surface for downstream adoption decisions.

## Entry Template

### K-YYYYMMDD-NNN
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Change:** One-sentence statement of what changed.
- **Rationale:** Why the change was made.
- **Scope:** Files or workflows affected.
- **Companion Artifacts:** Optional paths to changed-file bundles or convenience artifacts. These support review but do not outrank canon.
- **Follow-up:** Required implementation or validation steps.

## Downstream Mirroring

- Downstream mirrors record one disposition per upstream entry: `Included`, `Excluded`, or `Deferred`.
- Mirrored downstream entries may add local adoption notes, but the upstream entry remains the authoritative source description.
